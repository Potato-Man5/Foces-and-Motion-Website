Forces & Free-Body Diagrams

Identify every push and pull: gravity, normal force, friction, tension. Drawing a free-body diagram helps clarify what matters in a situation.

Newton’s Laws — Why things accelerate

Newton’s 2nd law (ΣF = ma) links net force, mass, and acceleration — a core idea used for braking, pushing carts, and vehicle motion.

Friction in everyday life

Friction depends on surface types and normal force. It keeps us from slipping but can also make pushing heavy loads harder.

Work, Energy & Efficiency

Work (force × distance) and energy explain why smoother acceleration saves fuel and why lifting loads takes more effort.
